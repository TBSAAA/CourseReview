## 3. Emphasis

```c++
Bold **words**
Italic *words*
Bold and Italic ***words***
//Code highlighting ```code```

```

```c++

```

## 5. Lists

### Unordered

```c++
* Item 1
* Item 2
  * Item 2a
  * Item 2b
```

* Item 1
* Item 2
  * Item 2a
  * Item 2b

### Ordered

```c++
1. Item 1
1. Item 2
1. Item 3
   1. Item 3a
   1. Item 3b
```

1. Item 1
1. Item 2
1. Item 3
   1. Item 3a
   1. Item 3b
